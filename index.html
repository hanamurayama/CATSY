Catsy is a new food truck brand in Los Angeles and Orange County that makes unique, eye-catching cat-shaped sandwiches inspired by Japanese cat-shaped bread loaves and Korean-style toasts. The brand’s name is a combined word of “cat” and “sassy” and designed to remind customers of cats being able to act both “cute” and “humorous.”
